Practical HR systems for small organizations.

Meadowlark HR provides project-based support for small businesses and nonprofits that want their people systems to be clear, organized, and easy to navigate. Services focus on practical HR infrastructure — including employee handbooks, onboarding processes, and benefits communication — that help bring structure and consistency to everyday HR operations.

Whether you're building a new handbook or looking to organize core HR processes, Meadowlark HR focuses on clearly scoped projects that make HR systems easier to manage and understand.

Our Services

Employee Handbook Development

Create a clear, thoughtfully organized employee handbook tailored to your organization. Meadowlark HR helps structure policies, clarify expectations, and organize information so employees and managers can easily understand how your workplace operates.

This service focuses on practical, well-organized documentation that supports day-to-day clarity while leaving room for legal review where appropriate.

Onboarding & Hiring Process Setup

A well-designed onboarding experience helps new employees feel supported from their first day. This service helps organizations organize the foundational processes that guide hiring and onboarding, including documentation workflows, checklists, and communication materials.

The goal is to create systems that are consistent, easy to manage, and welcoming for new team members.

HR Systems Organization

As organizations grow, HR information can become scattered across documents, folders, and systems. Meadowlark HR helps bring structure and clarity to these materials by organizing core HR documentation and processes.

Projects may include organizing policies, benefits information, templates, and internal HR resources so teams can navigate them more easily.
